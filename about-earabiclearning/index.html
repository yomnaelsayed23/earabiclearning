About Us

Mission Statement

To provide the best Arabic language education service worldwide at an affordable price and always focus on the needs of our customers, offering them an effective and enjoyable Arabic language learning experience.

 

 

Our Goals

Our goals are to train non-Arabic speakers to become proficient in speaking, writing, and reading the Arabic language.

This will enable them to study in Arabic Universities, or teach Arabic as a second language so they can obtain employment in translation services as well as other employment in Arab countries.

 

Our Vision


eArabicLearning.com is an Arabic learning academy for students all over the world that want to improve their Arabic in an affordable, fast and effective way.
We offer live online teaching with our group of qualified native teachers which will make your learning fun and effective.

With a unique combination of skill and experience, our team has created an Arabic language course that enables students to learn the language "live", via ZOOM, Skype or Google meet from anywhere in the world.

Our sessions are conducted through video conferencing. Students can see the teacher via webcam, and can speak with the teacher and other class members with their computer microphones. Lessons are transmitted using multimedia applications on the computer screen and are also explained using an electronic whiteboard and pen. Classes are a combination of content that has been written specifically for online learning, and they use advanced technologies for distance learning via the Internet.

 eArabicLearning.com is an exceptional language-learning experience which takes advantage of the convenience and effectiveness of online learning and brings the Arabic language to every corner of the globe.
 

Teaching methods and strategies we rely on:

 

- Teaching Arabic to Kids through Arabic games .

- Teaching Arabic to adults through linguistic immersion programs .

-  Islamic education through short Islamic stories .

- Teaching Arabic to young children through entertainment .

- Teaching Qur’an recitation through memorization and mastery .

 

eArabicLearning relies on the American Council on the Teaching of Foreign Languages (ACTFL) proficiency guidelines in teaching Arabic language because it is a widely recognized and respected framework for assessing language proficiency. ACTFL guidelines provide a clear and objective standard for measuring language proficiency in four key areas: speaking, writing, reading, and listening.

 

By basing their Arabic language curriculum on the ACTFL guidelines, eArabicLearning is able to ensure that their courses are aligned with industry standards and that their students are receiving a high-quality education that meets their needs. The ACTFL guidelines are widely used by language educators and institutions across the United States, and they are recognized as a reliable and accurate way to measure language proficiency.

 

 

Additionally, the ACTFL guidelines provide a framework for assessing language proficiency that is consistent across languages, which makes it easier for students to transfer their language skills to other languages if they choose to do so in the future. Overall, by relying on the ACTFL guidelines, eArabicLearning is able to provide a rigorous and effective Arabic language education that prepares their students for success in a globalized world

Here's a breakdown of what eArabicLearning offers at each level, based on the ACTFL proficiency guidelines:

 

Beginner Levels: eArabicLearning offers courses that focus on building basic vocabulary and grammar skills. Students will develop their listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills through simple conversations and short interactions in the language. They will learn how to produce simple sentences and short phrases related to familiar topics, understand short, simple messages, recognize and understand some familiar words and basic phrases in context, and write simple sentences and short texts on familiar topics.

 

Intermediate Levels: eArabicLearning offers courses that build on the foundation developed at the beginner levels. Students will expand their knowledge of the language and develop greater proficiency in listening, speaking, reading, and writing. They will learn how to engage in extended conversations on a range of topics, understand the main ideas and some details of longer spoken messages, read and understand longer texts on a variety of topics, and write paragraphs and short compositions on a variety of topics.

 

Advanced Levels: eArabicLearning offers courses that aim to achieve near-native proficiency in the target language. Students will communicate effectively in a wide range of situations and on a variety of topics, including abstract and complex ones. They will have a deep understanding of the language and be able to engage in advanced-level communication, including the ability to express complex ideas and opinions, analyze and interpret information, and communicate with a high degree of accuracy and precision.

 

Overall, eArabicLearning's courses aim to provide a comprehensive learning experience that is based on the ACTFL proficiency guidelines. Students will have access to a range of learning resources, including video lessons, interactive exercises, and one-on-one tutoring sessions with experienced Arabic language instructors. The goal is to help students achieve their language learning goals and become proficient in Arabic, whether for personal or professional reasons.
