Our Arabic Language Online Teachers

         eArabicLearning's Arabic teachers are highly qualified and have graduated from prestigious institutions such as Al-Azhar University. Al-Azhar University is one of the oldest and most reputable universities in the Arab world, known for its rigorous academic standards in Arabic language, literature, Sharia, and Islamic law. It is a highly respected institution that produces scholars and experts in the Arabic language.

In addition to their undergraduate degrees, the Arabic teachers at eArabicLearning also hold a Master's degree in the Department of  Arabic Language Teaching Methods, which is offered by the Education faculty at Al-Azhar University. This specialized program focuses on developing effective teaching techniques and strategies for teaching Arabic as a foreign language to non-native speakers.

 

The combination of their academic background and practical experience in teaching Arabic as a foreign language allows eArabicLearning's teachers to deliver high-quality lessons to their students. They are able to tailor their teaching methods to the needs of individual learners, whether they are beginners or advanced students.

 

Overall, eArabicLearning's commitment to hiring highly qualified Arabic teachers from top universities like Al-Azhar University is a testament to their dedication to providing the best possible Arabic language education to their students.

Our certified Arabic tutors with many certifications and degrees in teaching Arabic for Non-native Arabic speakers will guide you through your online Arabic classes to the level of fluency in Arabic. Our Arabic Teachers have many hands-on years of experience of teaching online Arabic courses (modern standard Arabic (MSA) - Classical Arabic - Egyptain dialect - Quran and Tajweed). Our teachers are always available to give you online Arabic lessons VIA Skype or Zoom. The teachers have extensive knowledge in teaching Arabic classes for kids. Male and female teachers are also available.
