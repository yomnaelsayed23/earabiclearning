Arabic Courses Programs

 

eArabicLearning is an online school for online Arabic language teaching, offering LIVE lessons taught by certified and professional teachers from Cairo.

The unique, interdisciplinary program was designed by a team of linguistic experts, distance learning specialists and programmers, who have dedicated themselves to the goal of teaching the Arabic language Online, using the most recent tools.

eArabicLearning provides live one-to-one online  Arabic lessons for students to learn Arabic worldwide. Our Arabic lessons are customized to meet a variety of needs: religious, business, travel, daily life, and so on. Our Arabic teachers are professionals and experienced in helping individuals belongs to different backgrounds and age groups.

Our professional services guarantee that you will get the best experience in learning Arabic!

 

FROM BEGINNER TO ADVANCE YOU WILL SURPRISE YOURSELF!

You can make rapid progress towards fluency with a course at your level, delivered to your needs. Your progress is monitored from the first day and you would receive regular feedback throughout your course. You will be impressed at just how quickly your confidence grows up as your Arabic language skills improve, immersed in real-life situations and interactions within a wholly Arabic speaking environment. In delivering the class, the eArabicLearning.com teaching staff uses PDF ebook, short films,education games, images and audio files

 

Time of class

Time of class is negotiable according to the teacher and student schedule
You have your own choice to have 3 or 5 lessons per week (regular or intensive) with your own private teacher (each lesson lasts between one and two hours). In addition, you would normally be given between one and two hours' home work to prepare the next day's lessons.

 

Level estimation

On the first day, you will be given a level assessment and listening comprehension and your oral fluency will be assessed. The teacher will then design a Personal Study Plan with you to fulfill your objectives by making the most effective use of your lesson time. Out of lessons you will have the opportunity to practice what you have learnt.

Level estimation is the process of determining a student's current level of proficiency in the Arabic language. eArabicLearning uses a level estimation process to evaluate the student's listening comprehension and oral fluency, which helps the teacher to determine the student's strengths and weaknesses and tailor the course content and teaching methods to the student's specific needs.

 

The level estimation process typically begins with a placement test, which measures the student's knowledge of vocabulary, grammar, and comprehension. The test is designed to evaluate the student's understanding of the language and identify any areas where the student may need extra support or reinforcement. The test results will be used to place the student in the appropriate course level, based on the student's proficiency in the language.

 

After the level estimation, the teacher will work with the student to design a Personal Study Plan (PSP) that outlines the student's learning objectives, target areas for improvement, and a schedule of course content and activities that are tailored to the student's level and needs. The PSP will also include a schedule of homework assignments and other activities that are intended to support the student's learning outside of class time.

 

The level estimation and PSP will be reviewed and updated regularly by the teacher throughout the course to ensure that the student is making steady progress towards their objectives and to adjust the course content and activities as needed. This will help the student to make the most effective use of their lesson time and reach their goals as soon as possible.

 

In summary, eArabicLearning uses a level estimation process to determine the student's current level of proficiency in the Arabic language, which allows the teacher to tailor the course content and teaching methods to the student's specific needs. This process is designed to ensure that the student's course content and activities are tailored to their specific level and to provide regular feedback and support to help the student make rapid progress towards fluency.
