  • Mastering the formal or religious speech
  • Reading books or magazine

Quranic Arabic course

 Quranic Arabic course is a course that teaches the language of the Quran, which is classical Arabic. It is designed to help learners understand the Quran and its meaning in the original language. The course covers the grammar, vocabulary, and syntax of Arabic, as well as the rules of pronunciation.

 

The goal of a Quranic Arabic course is to enable learners to read and understand the Quran directly, without relying on translations. This is important because the Quran is considered the central text of Islam and is revered by Muslims as the literal word of God.

 

In addition to the language itself, a Quranic Arabic course may also cover topics related to the Quran, such as its history, context, and interpretation. This can help learners deepen their understanding of the Quran and its significance in Islamic culture and society.

Quranic Arabic courses are offered by many Islamic educational universities like Al-Azhar university and eArabicLearning platform. They are typically taught by qualified teachers who have expertise in both Arabic and Islamic studies.

Quranic Arabic course   at eArabicLearning typically covers the following syllabus:

 

Arabic Alphabet: Introduction to the Arabic Alphabet, pronunciation, and basic writing skills.

 

Grammar: Introduction to Arabic grammar, including nouns, verbs, pronouns, adjectives, and prepositions.

 

Vocabulary: Building Arabic vocabulary through common Quranic words and phrases.

Reading and comprehension: Developing skills in reading and comprehending Quranic texts.

 

Quranic Tafsir: Introduction to the interpretation and explanation of Quranic verses, including their historical and cultural contexts.

 

Tajweed: Study of the rules and principles of Tajweed (the proper pronunciation and recitation of the Quran).

 

Memorization: Memorizing and reciting selected Quranic verses and chapters.

 

Arabic Conversation: Developing basic conversational skills in Arabic with a focus on Quranic language.

 

Islamic Terminology: Introduction to the Islamic terms and concepts commonly found in Quranic texts.

 

Arabic Literature: Introduction to classical Arabic literature with a focus on Quranic literature.

 

Overall, the Quranic Arabic course aims to provide students with the skills and knowledge needed to read and understand the Quran in its original Arabic language.
